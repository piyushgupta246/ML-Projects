About Heart Disease

Heart disease refers to a range of conditions that affect the heart, including coronary artery disease, arrhythmias, and heart defects. Common symptoms include chest pain, shortness of breath, and fatigue. Early detection is critical for effective treatment and prevention.

This tool predicts the likelihood of heart disease based on key health metrics, enabling timely action to safeguard your heart health.
